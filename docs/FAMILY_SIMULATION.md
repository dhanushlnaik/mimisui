# Family Simulation and Seasons

## Scope

Family Simulation extends relationship progression with competitive loops:

- simulation ladder
- duel history
- milestones
- seasonal resets/claims

## Season Flow

- Active season tracks ranked outcomes.
- Admin can start/end season.
- End of season locks progression for payouts/archive.
- New season starts cleanly with controlled reset/recompute tools.

## Milestones

Milestone flows include progression targets and streak achievements.

Examples:

- cumulative sim wins
- rank points thresholds
- streak-based bonus achievements

## Claims

Season and achievement claims are persisted to avoid duplicate rewards.

## Leaderboard

Leaderboard rank sorting generally follows:

1. primary score metric
2. tie-break by total wins/dates/streak depending on board
3. deterministic fallback on stable identity
